Executive Summary

The first BPI-certified compostable warmth poncho for marathon race start lines — replacing 35,000+ garbage bags discarded at every major marathon.

The Problem

Every major marathon generates 35,000–60,000 single-use warmth products at the start line. NYC Marathon alone generates 26 tons of start-line textile waste. Zero compostable alternatives exist. Full analysis: P0A.

The Solution

A BPI-certified compostable warmth poncho (NatureFlex NM + PLA spunbond) with patent-pending tear-away design. Composts in 12–24 weeks at commercial facilities. Includes end-to-end composting logistics. Material spec: P2A, Design: P2B.

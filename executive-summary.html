Executive Summary

The first BPI-certified compostable warmth poncho designed specifically for marathon race start lines — replacing the 35,000+ garbage bags discarded at every major marathon.

The Problem

Every major marathon generates 35,000–60,000 single-use warmth products at the start line. Runners wear garbage bags, donated sweatshirts, or Heatsheets Mylar blankets for 30–60 minutes, then discard them. NYC Marathon alone generates 26 tons of start-line textile waste. Zero compostable alternatives exist in the market.

The Solution

A BPI-certified compostable warmth poncho (NatureFlex NM metallized cellulose + PLA spunbond) with patent-pending tear-away design. Provides equivalent warmth to current solutions, composts in 12–24 weeks at commercial facilities, and includes end-to-end composting logistics for race directors.

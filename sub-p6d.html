Complete SEO & Content Strategy: 10-Keyword Target Map, 4 Cornerstone Blog Posts, Race-Specific Landing Page System, 6 Editorial Submission Targets, and 6-Channel Backlink Acquisition Plan

March 2026 | Confidential

Executive Summary

SEO is the only marketing channel that compounds. A Facebook ad stops generating traffic the moment the budget runs out. An SEO-optimized blog post generates traffic for YEARS. A race-specific landing page captures seasonal search traffic every fall and spring without any incremental spend. The content created in Weeks 12–18 will drive organic traffic in Month 6, Month 12, and Month 36.

This document builds the complete organic search strategy: 10 target keywords mapped across product, informational, and race-specific intent, 4 cornerstone blog posts establishing topical authority, a scalable race-specific landing page system capturing thousands of seasonal searches per major marathon, editorial submission pitches to 6 running and outdoor media outlets, and a 6-channel backlink acquisition plan.

1. SEO Keyword Strategy: 10 Target Keywords

Keywords are organized in 3 layers: PRIMARY keywords (exact product + problem-aware terms), LONG-TAIL keywords (specific queries with high conversion intent), and RACE-SPECIFIC keywords (massive seasonal volume tied to marathon dates). The strategy targets LOW competition first.

1.1 Keyword Research Tools

FREE: Google Search Console (actual queries after 2–4 weeks), Google Keyword Planner (volumes, free with Ads account), AnswerThePublic (question discovery). PAID ($29–$49/month): Ahrefs Lite or Helium 10 for difficulty scores, competitor analysis, backlink monitoring. RECOMMENDATION: free tools for Season 1, add Ahrefs Month 3–6.

1.2 On-Page SEO Checklist

For EVERY page: (1) Title tag: primary keyword + brand, <60 chars. (2) Meta description: keyword + compelling copy, <155 chars. (3) H1: 1 per page with keyword. (4) URL slug: short, keyword-rich. (5) Image alt text: descriptive with keyword where natural. (6) Internal links: 2–3 per page to relevant site pages. (7) Schema markup: Product schema on product pages for rich snippets. Use JSON-LD for SEO Shopify app ($0–$10/month).

2.1 Blog Publishing Checklist

Before every post: (1) On-page SEO complete per Section 1.2. (2) Internal links to 2+ site pages. (3) External links to 1–2 authoritative sources (BPI, NOAA, EPA). (4) 2–3 images with alt text. (5) CTA at end (shop or request sample kit). (6) Share on all social channels with UTM links. (7) Feature in next Klaviyo email. (8) Submit URL to Search Console for indexing.

2.2 Ongoing Content Calendar

Post-launch: 1–2 new posts per month. Topics: race recaps (“How We Composted 450 lbs at [Race]”), seasonal guides (“Winter Running Warmth Checklist”), sustainability features (“5 Compostable Products for Runners”), product updates (“Introducing the 3-Pack”). Consistent publishing builds topical authority over time.

5. Backlink Acquisition: 6 Channels

Backlinks are the #1 off-page SEO factor. Running authority links + composting authority links = definitive source for compostable running products in Google’s view. Target: 10–20 quality backlinks in 6 months.

Channel Strategy
Guest posts on running blogs Pitch 10–20 running blogs (DR 20–50): “5 Ways to Make Race Day More Sustainable,” “The Environmental Cost of Running Events.” Expect 3–5 acceptances. Each post includes: author bio link to brand.com + 1–2 contextual content links.
Sustainability blog features Pitch TreeHugger (DR 80+), EcoWatch, Green Matters, Sustainable Jungle: “First BPI-certified race poncho — feature in sustainable product roundup?” A TreeHugger mention = massive SEO + audience alignment.
Race director resources Create brand.com/resources: downloadable composting guide, sustainability calculator (P5C), waste audit template. Pitch to Running USA, RRCA, RunSignUp. Earns PASSIVE backlinks as directors share.
Pilot race press coverage Press release to local media per race city after each pilot. “[City] Marathon Uses Compostable Ponchos, Diverts [X] lbs.” Local newspaper/TV sites provide DR 30–70 backlinks + press screenshots for social proof.
Composting industry publications Pitch BioCycle (DR 55+), US Composting Council, Compost Magazine: “New BPI-certified product entering event waste stream.” Niche-relevant composting authority links are highly valued by Google.
Strava Club + community Create branded Strava Club. Club description links to brand.com. Posts link to blog articles. Nofollow links but drive: referral traffic, brand awareness, and social signals supporting SEO.

5.1 Backlink Tracking

Google Sheet: Date | Source URL | Domain | DR | Anchor Text | Target Page | Status | Method. Monitor with Ahrefs Lite ($29/month) monthly for: new links, lost links, toxic links. TARGET: 10–20 DR 20+ backlinks by Month 6. 30–50 by Month 12. Each quality backlink improves ranking for ALL pages site-wide.

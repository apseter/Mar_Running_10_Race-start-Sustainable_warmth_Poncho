7. Final Verdict

Once these are addressed, P1 will be one of the most comprehensive brand development packages for a pre-launch DTC product at this investment level.

The most important next step: before moving to P2 (Product Design & Engineering), fix Gap #8 (pitch deck copy) and Gap #10 (crisis response). These are the two items most likely to be needed DURING P2 execution, as material suppliers and race directors will request the pitch deck and the brand must be prepared for public-facing sustainability claims from Day 1 of B2B outreach.
